# Formal Astrolabe Category

Lean 4 formalization of the astrolabe category theory, mechanically verified with Mathlib.

## Files

### Signature.lean

Defines the astrolabe signature $\Sigma = (O, M, h)$:

$$
\Sigma = (O, M, s, t, h, \text{info}_O, \text{info}_M)
$$

where $h : O + M \to \text{String}$ is an injective global identifier.

Also defines `SignatureMorphism` — maps between signatures preserving source and target.

### FreeCategory.lean

The astrolabe category $\mathcal{A}(\Sigma) := \text{Paths}(\Sigma.O)$ — the free category on the quiver induced by $\Sigma$.

**Proposition 3.2**: Well-definedness (four category axioms).

**Proposition 3.3**: Representability — any prefunctor lifts uniquely to a functor.

### Quotient.lean

Quotient categories $\mathcal{A}(\Sigma) / {\sim}$ where $\sim$ is a congruence.

**Proposition 4.3**: Congruence compatibility with composition.

**Example 5.4**: Any functor induces a valid congruence (content addressing).

### Universe.lean

Enrichment and quotient closure in the universe of astrolabe categories.

**Proposition 5.2**: Enrichment closure.

**Proposition 5.3**: Quotient closure — functors respecting a congruence factor through the quotient.

**Corollary 5.5**: Content addressing compatibility.

### SignatureCategory.lean

Signatures form a category **Sig**. The free category construction is functorial:

$$
\text{Free}(\text{id}_S) = \text{id}_{\mathcal{A}(S)}, \quad \text{Free}(\psi \circ \varphi) = \text{Free}(\varphi) \circ \text{Free}(\psi)
$$

### Functors/Import.lean

**Theorem 7.1**: Any signature morphism preserving source and target extends to a functor between free categories.

### Functors/NaturalTransformation.lean

Natural transformations between functors on $\mathcal{A}(\Sigma)$ — determined by components on generators.

### Functors/Rendering.lean, Layout.lean, Presentation.lean

Concrete target categories ($\mathcal{V}$, $\mathcal{P}$, $\mathcal{U}_{\text{ui}}$) and functors into them.

### Functors/MathDomain.lean, Timestamp.lean, NetworkAnalysis.lean

Enrichment functors — identity on categorical structure, extension on info records.
